In relation to my exam in social and cultural dynamics in cognition I want to investigate game theory and behavior dynamics.

In this project you will be playing an ultimatum game with other participants. You will be asked at some point of the experiment to share information about yourself with another participant. You only have to share information you feel comfortable with, and you are in complete control over what you choose to share.
